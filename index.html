Conversion Formulas

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius:

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Key Temperature Points:

  • Absolute Zero: -273.15°C = -459.67°F
  • Human Body Temperature: 37°C = 98.6°F
  • Room Temperature: 20-25°C = 68-77°F
